Last updated: February, 2026

At iDream Studios ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use WaterStreak.

Developer: iDream Studios

Website: https://www.idreamstudios.dev

Privacy contact: albertarredondoalfaro@gmail.com

WaterStreak is designed to minimize data collection.

We may process app usage data (for example, basic interaction events).

We may process diagnostic and crash data.

We may process purchase/subscription status managed by Apple (StoreKit).

3. Purposes of Processing

We process data to operate and maintain the app.

We process data to improve performance, stability, and user experience.

We process data to comply with legal obligations when required.

4. Legal Bases

Where data protection laws apply, we rely on performance of the service.

We rely on legitimate interests (security, reliability, product improvement).

We rely on consent where required for specific features, for example notifications/tracking.

5. Third Parties

We may use third-party services for analytics, diagnostics, notifications, and Apple-managed in-app payments/subscriptions.

If rewarded ads are enabled in the future, this policy will be updated to identify the ad provider and related data processing.

6. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, or objection.

Contact us at: albertarredondoalfaro@gmail.com

8. Children

WaterStreak is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent age in your jurisdiction). If we learn that we processed children's data without proper basis, we will delete it.

9. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at: https://www.idreamstudios.dev/privacy
