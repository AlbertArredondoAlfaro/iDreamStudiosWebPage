Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

iDream Studios is committed to protecting your privacy. Our iOS apps are designed to collect as little information as possible while still providing a reliable and enjoyable experience.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or precise location unless a specific app feature explicitly requires it. If an app includes analytics or crash reporting, the data is anonymized and used only to improve performance and stability.

How We Use Data

Any anonymized usage data or crash reports are used solely to enhance app quality, diagnose issues, and improve the user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Limited, anonymized data may be processed by Apple services as part of app distribution and standard analytics.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please refer to the app listing on the App Store for the official developer contact details.
